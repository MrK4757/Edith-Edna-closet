📦 Shipping Policy

How we deliver your favorite fashion items to your doorstep

Last Updated: January 1, 2026

🚚 Delivery Areas

Edith & Edna Closet currently ships to all regions within Ghana. We are working on expanding to other West African countries soon.

📍 Current Delivery Zones:
• Greater Accra Region
• Ashanti Region (Kumasi metropolitan area)
• Western North Region (Sefwi Wiawso and surrounding areas)
• Western Region (Takoradi, Sekondi)
• Central Region (Cape Coast)
• Eastern Region (Koforidua)
• All other regions via partner courier services

⏱️ Processing Time

Order processing time: 1-3 business days (Monday to Friday, excluding public holidays)

During this time, we verify your order, check product availability, package your items, and prepare them for shipment.

⚠️ Note: Orders placed on weekends or public holidays will be processed on the next business day.

🚛 Delivery Time & Rates

Delivery times vary by location. We partner with reliable courier services, including:

  • Red Star Express - Nationwide delivery
  • DHL Ghana - Express delivery (Accra & Kumasi)
  • Local dispatch riders - Same-day delivery in the Sefwi Wiawso area

📱 Order Tracking

Once your order is shipped, you will receive:

  1. SMS notification with tracking number and courier details
  2. Email confirmation with tracking link (if email provided)
  3. WhatsApp update for customers who provide a WhatsApp number

You can track your order status by contacting us via WhatsApp at (+233) 540556040 with your order number.

📦 Delivery Process

What to expect:

  • Before delivery: Our courier will call to confirm your availability and delivery time
  • At delivery: You'll receive a call from the dispatch rider when they arrive
  • For Cash on Delivery: Please have exact change ready as riders may not carry large amounts of change
  • For Mobile Money: Ensure you have completed payment before delivery

Failed Delivery Attempts:

If delivery fails after two (2) attempts due to:

  • Wrong or incomplete address provided
  • Recipient not available at the provided location
  • Phone number unreachable or incorrect

The order will be returned to our warehouse, and you will be contacted to arrange redelivery at an additional fee of 15 GHS.

⚠️ Important Notes

  • Address Accuracy: Please double-check your delivery address before submitting your order. We are not responsible for orders delivered to incorrect addresses provided by customers.
  • Delivery Delays: While we strive to meet delivery timelines, unforeseen circumstances (weather, traffic, public holidays, courier issues) may cause delays. We will notify you of any significant delays.
  • P.O. Box Addresses: We cannot deliver to P.O. Box addresses. Please provide a physical address with landmark for easy navigation.
  • Working Hours: Deliveries are made Monday-Saturday, 9:00 AM - 6:00 PM. Sunday and public holiday deliveries available for express service only (additional fee may apply).

🌍 International Shipping

We currently do not offer international shipping.
